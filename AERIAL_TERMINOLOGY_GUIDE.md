### 3. **Sequences** ğŸ¬

### 4. **Inversions** ğŸ™ƒ

**Why it's a separate interest:**
- Not everyone likes/can do inversions
- Some people have medical restrictions
- Others LOVE the feeling of being upside down

**Safety note:**
- Requires proper progression
- Not for complete beginners
- Important to tag correctly!
