# Privacy Policy for Aerial Tricks

**Last Updated:** November 22, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to Aerial Tricks ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Aerial Tricks mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Email address**
- **Full name**
- **Password** (encrypted and securely stored)
- **Profile information** (skill level, experience)
- **Profile photo** (optional)

### 2. Authentication Information

When you sign in using third-party services:
- **Google Sign-In:** Email address, name, profile picture
- **Apple Sign-In:** Email address (or private relay email), name
- **Facebook Login:** Email address, name, profile picture

### 3. Usage Data

We automatically collect:
- **Tutorial viewing history** (which tutorials you've watched)
- **Progress tracking data** (mastered tutorials, favorites, completion status)
- **Tutorial ratings and reviews**
- **Collection browsing activity**
- **App usage analytics** (features used, time spent)

### 4. User-Generated Content

You may provide:
- **Progress photos** (uploaded to track your aerial yoga progress)
- **Community posts** (text and images you share)
- **Comments and interactions** with other users' content
- **Tutorial reviews and ratings**

### 5. Technical Information

We collect:
- **Device information** (device type, operating system, app version)
- **IP address** and general location (country/region)
- **Log data** (access times, errors, performance data)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide and improve our service:**
   - Deliver tutorial content
   - Track your progress and achievements
   - Personalize recommendations
   - Enable social features (community posts, comments)

2. **Authenticate and secure your account:**
   - Verify your identity
   - Prevent unauthorized access
   - Detect and prevent fraud

3. **Communicate with you:**
   - Send welcome emails
   - Notify you of new tutorials
   - Respond to support requests
   - Send important updates (optional marketing emails with your consent)

4. **Analyze and improve:**
   - Understand how users interact with the app
   - Improve app performance and features
   - Fix bugs and technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Infrastructure

We use **Supabase** (a secure, SOC 2 Type II certified backend platform) to store your data:
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt)
- Database access is restricted and monitored
- Regular security audits are performed

### Content Storage

- **Video tutorials** are stored securely and delivered via CDN
- **User-uploaded photos** are stored in secure cloud storage
- **Personal data** is stored in encrypted databases

### Security Measures

We implement:
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security updates
- Access control and monitoring
- Data backup and recovery procedures

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT sell your personal information.

We may share your information only in these cases:

1. **With your consent:** When you explicitly agree to share information

2. **Public content:** 
   - Community posts and comments you make are visible to other users
   - Your username and profile picture (if set) are visible to other users

3. **Service providers:**
   - Supabase (database and authentication)
   - Cloud storage providers (for videos and images)
   - Analytics services (anonymized data only)

4. **Legal requirements:**
   - To comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes
   - To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
   - To enforce our Terms of Service

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### 1. Access Your Data
- View all personal information we have about you
- Export your data in a portable format

### 2. Correct Your Data
- Update your profile information anytime
- Correct inaccurate information

### 3. Delete Your Data
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- We will permanently delete your data within 30 days (except where required by law)

### 4. Control Your Privacy
- Choose what information to share
- Control who sees your posts and progress
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Manage third-party login connections

### 5. Data Portability
- Download your data (tutorials watched, progress, posts)
- Transfer your data to another service

## How to Exercise Your Rights

To access, update, or delete your data:
1. Go to **Profile** ‚Üí **Settings** in the app
2. Use the "Delete Account" or "Export Data" options

## Children's Privacy

Aerial Tricks is intended for users aged **13 and above**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
